Laneika Lyons '19, a Junior Computer Science student at Medgar Evers College (MEC) of the City of New York.

She currently serves as the corresponding secretary for MEC's Student Government Association, president of the ACM Club, and vice chair of technology fee affairs of the CUNY University Student Senate.


While developing her passion of advocating for the voiceless, she has simultaneously worked on technical projects such as participating in the Google Ignites CS programs (a program where computer science university students around the US teach local K-12 students to learn coding), creating wearable arduino, developing a travelling mobile app and a preschool learning app, creating a virtual reality gaming app prototype during a hackathon, and learning unity in order to create virtual reality humanoid robots.
